Shift 03 — The profile

The old sales archetype doesn't win anymore.

We interviewed hundreds of top performers. The people thriving today look nothing like the people the industry used to reward.

The old archetype
  • Persuaders and hard closers
  • Door-knockers and cold-callers
  • Smile-and-dial personalities
  • Loudest voice in the room
  • Memorize the pitch, repeat on loop
Who wins now
  • People motivated by helping others
  • Professional listeners
  • People who excel at social media
  • A teacher's instinct — make complex ideas simple

Common questions

Everything people want to know — upfront.

Is this commission-only?
Compensation is discussed in detail with the hiring manager during the interview process. It's a combination structure that includes Commission, Residuals, Bonuses, and Stocks.
Do I need a license?
You'll need a state health and life insurance license. Most agencies sponsor the course and cover the cost. It takes most people one to two weeks to complete.
Will I have to cold call?
No. Inbound inquiries are routed to you by zip code, and modern agencies coach you on generating warm introductions from your existing network. Cold calling is not part of the modern playbook.
Do I need a sales background?
No — in fact, people without a traditional sales background often do well here because the platform handles most of the heavy lifting. What matters is listening well, being organized, and being coachable.
Can I work remote?
Yes. You can run your entire practice from video, meet clients locally in person, or mix both. Your choice.
Can I do this part-time?
Yes. Agencies in our network have full-time agents, part-time agents, and agents running this alongside another business.

Tell us about you.

We'll review your information against the agencies in our network and route you to the best fit. An agency lead will reach out within one business day to introduce themselves and schedule a short conversation.
